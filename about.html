My name is Lauren Flannery and I am an undergraduate student at the University of Florida.

When I came to college, I threw myself into the journalism school determined to do it all. Throughout my time at UF, I dabbled in radio production, television production, editorial writing and reporting all outside of my course load of two degrees. UF's journalism school offered so many great cutting-edge resources and fun oppurtunities that focusing on what I wanted to pursue in communications was difficult.

I'm pursuing my dual-degree in journalism and business administration. After I graduate, I'm excited to make a name for myself in the world of communications.
